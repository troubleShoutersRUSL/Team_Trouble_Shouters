About Us

We are Team Trouble Shouters, a group of passionate students pursuing a BSc degree in the Department of Computing, Faculty of Applied Science, at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. Our website focuses on developing a skill-based job recommendation system for undergraduates. Our website provides a comprehensive platform for soft skill ssessment and career guidance, offering quizzes to evaluate skills.Based on these assessments, users receive personalized career recommendations, can track their progress, and access resources to enhance their skills. Our vision is to empower undergraduates with the knowledge and confidence to pursue careers that match their skills and passions, bridging the gap between academic learning and professional success.
